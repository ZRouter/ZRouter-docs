# Flash

```
5VT1310-EVB# tftpboot 00800000 Buffalo_WZR2-G300N.zimage
5VT1310-EVB# erase 0x1F010000 0x1F3CFFFF
5VT1310-EVB# cp.b 800000 0x1F010000 $(filesize)
```

also setenv to ipaddr and serverip for your network.
